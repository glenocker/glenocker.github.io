    Explore my mechanical engineering projects and experiences as well as my hobbies outside of engineering.

    About Me

    I'm a mechanical engineering student at California State University, Fullerton passionate about design, fabrication, and innovation. This portfolio showcases my projects, skills, and hobbies.

    Projects

    Toyota Tacoma Camping Bed Conversion

    Designed a camping bed insert for a Toyota Tacoma that has different storage spaces, and allows for a mattress or sleeping bag to be placed on top.

    Solar Oven

    Created an oven that uses energy from the sun to cook a biscuit.

    Single-Axis Solar Tracker

    Designed a solar tracker to keep a solar panel always angled towards the sun.

    Dirt Bike Maintenance

    I do the majority of work on my dirt bikes myself. I've rebuilt the top end of my engine, rebuilt carburetors, adjusted valve timing, and more.

    1983 Porsche 944 Restoration

    Restoring a 1983 Porsche 944 that has not run since the '90s.

    Hobbies

    Outside of engineering, I'm passionate about dirt bikes, videography, and photography. I decided to combine my love for all of them and now, if I'm not riding a dirt bike, I'm filming one, capturing motion and the energy of motorsports through my lens.

    Videography

    I film using my Sony FX30 and edit with Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects. I focus on action, color grading, and storytelling through motion.
